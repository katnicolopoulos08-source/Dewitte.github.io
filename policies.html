Financing Policy

At GreenSpark Cleaning Co., we offer flexible financing options for businesses and organizations purchasing products in bulk. Qualified customers may choose a monthly payment plan to make large orders more affordable and convenient.

Delivery Policy

Orders are typically processed within 1-2 business days and delivered within 5-7 business days. Free shipping is available on orders over 50$, and customers will receive tracking information once their order has been shipped.

Return Policy

Customer satisfaction is important to us. Unopened products may be returned within 30 days of purchase for a full refund, and damaged or defective items will be replaced at no additional cost.
