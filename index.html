About Us

GreenSpark Cleaning Co. was founded in 2020 by Emma Carter to create safe, effective, and environmentally friendly cleaning products. What started as a small business selling refillable all-purpose cleaners has grown into a company offering a full line of eco-friendly household cleaning supplies.

Why Choose Us?

Our products use plant-based ingredients, recyclable packaging, and refillable containers to reduce waste. We believe customers should not have to choose bewteen a clean home and a healthy planet.
